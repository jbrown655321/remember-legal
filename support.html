Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data including entries, threads, photos, and account information.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Remember, then tap Cancel. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data. Your entries and threads will be exported as a JSON file that you can save or share.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your data is encrypted in transit, protected by row-level security, and never used for advertising or AI model training. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

Does the app work offline?

Yes. You can capture entries offline and they'll sync automatically when you're back online.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. This will restore any active subscription linked to your Apple ID.
